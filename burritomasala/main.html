About Us

We are a food cart that specializes in the fusion of Mexican and Indian cusines into delicious burritos or bowls. We are a husband and wife duo who strive to make your creation a reality. There is no prefixed menu. Just select ingredients from our products list and design the perfect combination for your unique palette.

Design Process:

  1. Would you like a Burrito or Bowl?
  2. Choose your main ingredient (meat or veggie?)
  3. Choose your rice
  4. Fill out your burrito/bowl with toppings!
